ENVIRONMENT, HEALTH, AND SAFET

At CTC, the management is committed to the well-being of our employees, ensuring that they work and thrive in a safe and healthy environment. We prioritize comprehensive training for all project personnel and staff to maintain high safety standards across our worksites. Our dedication extends to safeguarding the welfare of every individual involved, regardless of their role or rank, ensuring a secure and supportive working environment for all.

SUSTAINABILITY

CTC is unwavering in its commitment to promoting a sustainable environment in alignment with the objectives of COP28 sustainability policy Dubai. We advocate for the highest standards of quality, safety, and environmental responsibility in every aspect of our operations. In support of the government’s initiative for green buildings, CTC is dedicated to partnering with clients to achieve these sustainability goals. We will take a leadership role in energy conservation and are committed to designing and implementing projects that incorporate green products and construction materials. Our focus is on delivering projects that not only meet but exceed industry standards for environmental stewardship, ensuring a positive impact on both our communities and the natural world.

Processes, Systems & Knowledge

In CTC, We understand that excellence can be achieved only through a systematic, consistent, and organized approach, and also through a well managed quality systems implemented at all levels of organization. CTC, with the commitment from partners and senior management, strive for excellence and maintain it by;

  • Employing the best people
  • Close site supervision and control, and
  • Adhering to our internal Quality Assurance and Quality Control Systems

Cost Control:

In CTC, We prefer mechanized than labour intensive works as this is essential in reducing costs and increasing the efficiency. We employ the best equipment operators and provide training to those that we see has a potential to excel in the same field.

Information Management:

In CTC Contracting we believe that the application of innovative and modern technologies would allow us to respond promptly to the rapid market changes as well as the massive construction boom in the United Arab Emirates.

Programme:

Our Planning Engineers are using the latest Primavera software. The Project Controls Department supports the Logistics, Cost, and the Construction Teams. The Planning Section extensively tracks the site progress, and reports to the whole team and to the Project Manager current status and any deviation from the baseline programme for an immediate mitigation.

Execution Planning:

During tender, CTC Contracting develops a comprehensive execution plan for the whole works. We plan our resource allocations, identify locations where major materials are to emanate, lead time for imported materials, as well as develop method statements for the major works. This tool serves as an input to our construction programme, as well as our Project Management Plan.

Quality and Environmental Management:

In CTC Contracting we are advocate in maintaining high quality end products, we are implementing all of our works in accordance with our Quality Assurance Programs and Quality Control Procedures.

Company’s Training Initiatives:
